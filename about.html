OUR HISTORY

Island Microfinance Bank Limited Is One Of The Leading Microfinance Bank With Its Corporate Office At 33, Moloney Street, Opposite Kam-Selem Building, Obalende. Island Microfinance Bank, Formerly Known As Island Community Bank Limited, Is Established By A Group Of Highly Professional People With Over 30 Years Individual Experience. Since Its Conversion To A Microfinance Bank, The Bank Has Consistently Been A Trail Blazer In The Microfinance Financial Institutions In The Business Of Service Quality And Exceptional Customer Service. The Bank Is Strategically Positioned To Offer Financial Services Support To Small And Micro-Enterprises In Nigeria.

Within Over Fifteen Years Of Business Operations, The Bank Has Grew From A Few Customers To Thousands Of Depositors, With An Efficient Workforce Handling Thousands Of Transactions Annually. The Bank Prides Itself As A Promise Keeper To Its Numerous Customers, With Testimonials All Abounding, In Efficient And Transparent Handling Of Their Deposits And In Loan Disbursement. This Proven By Ratings And Recommendations We Both Receive From Customers And Regulators.
